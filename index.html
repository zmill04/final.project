Biography

LeBron James, born December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio, is widely regarded the greatest basketball players of all time. Known for his exceptional skills, leadership, and versatility, he has played for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers, winning four NBA championships across his career.
